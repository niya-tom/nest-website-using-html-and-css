How We Add Value

NeST DIGITAL supports banks and financial institutions across the full value chain, from core transaction processing to digital channels and advanced analytics. Teams focus on building secure, high-performance systems that comply with regulatory standards while keeping customer experience at the center.

Engagements typically include re-platforming legacy systems, integrating payment gateways and wallets, and automating complex processes such as loan origination, KYC, and risk assessment. The goal is to enable faster go-to-market, lower operational cost, and improved transparency.

Key Solution Areas

  • Digital banking portals and mobile apps for retail, corporate, and SME customers.
  • Anti-fraud, AML, and transaction monitoring solutions with real-time alerts.
  • Data platforms for risk scoring, predictive analytics, and customer 360° views.
  • Cloud-native microservices and API gateways for open banking ecosystems.

Engagement Models

Delivery models are flexible, ranging from dedicated product engineering teams to outcome-based managed services. This allows banks and fintechs to scale engineering capacity quickly, while maintaining strong governance and quality controls.

Typical Use Cases

  • Real-time payment and settlement platforms.
  • Customer service automation with chat and bots.
