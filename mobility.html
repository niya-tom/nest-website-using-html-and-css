Mobility Solutions Overview

NeST DIGITAL works with OEMs, Tier-1 suppliers, and mobility providers to design and build software and electronics that power modern vehicles and mobility ecosystems. Areas of focus include EV systems, advanced driver assistance, telematics, and digital services that enhance safety, comfort, and convenience.

By combining embedded engineering with cloud, analytics, and UX capabilities, solutions enable connected, software-defined vehicles and mobility platforms that can be updated, optimized, and personalized over time.
