Locomotive Solutions Overview

NeST DIGITAL supports rolling stock manufacturers, operators, and rail system integrators with electronics and software that keep locomotives running reliably in demanding conditions. Solutions cover onboard control units, driver interfaces, event recorders, and asset monitoring systems across the fleet.

Engineering teams focus on real-time performance, robust communication between subsystems, and strong diagnostics capabilities, enabling timely intervention and minimizing unplanned downtime while improving overall safety and availability.
